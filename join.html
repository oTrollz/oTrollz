Why Join Cedar Grove Ambulance & Rescue Squad?

Becoming part of the Cedar Grove Ambulance & Rescue Squad means more than volunteering — it’s joining a family of dedicated neighbors who serve and protect nearly 13,000 residents. As a member, you’ll gain skills, experience, and friendships that last a lifetime.

Benefits of Joining

  • Training & Education: Free CPR/First Aid, EMT, and driver training opportunities. Continuous learning through drills, workshops, and community courses.
  • Certifications: Nationally recognized certifications that strengthen your professional and academic future. Hands-on experience that prepares you for careers in healthcare, public safety, and beyond.
  • Camaraderie: Join a close-knit team built on trust, teamwork, and shared purpose. Lifelong friendships with fellow volunteers who share your passion for service.
  • Community Service Hours: Earn meaningful volunteer service hours for school, college, or career requirements. Make a direct impact in your hometown by answering the call when your neighbors need it most.
  • Personal Growth: Build leadership, problem-solving, and decision-making skills under pressure. Discover the pride that comes from being there for someone in their greatest time of need.

Roles Available

  • EMT
  • Driver
  • Junior Member

FAQ for Applicants

Junior Members (15-17) are required to ride a minimum of 4 hours a week.

All probationary members must obtain their EMT certification within one year of joining.

Members over the age of 18 must ride 12 hours a week.
