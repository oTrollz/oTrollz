Emergency Response

We respond to a wide variety of emergencies including medical calls, trauma, motor vehicle accidents, and more. Our volunteers are trained to provide rapid and professional care when every second counts.

Community Outreach

CGARS is active in the community, offering CPR classes, first aid demonstrations, and standby coverage at local events. We believe education and preparedness save lives.

Mutual Aid

We work closely with nearby towns to ensure comprehensive emergency coverage throughout the region. Mutual aid agreements allow us to assist neighboring communities and receive support when needed.
